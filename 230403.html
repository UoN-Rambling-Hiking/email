Together, we've expanded our community to its largest size in several years, exploring new heights while reviving cherished traditions. My Highlights of the Year

Leading Walks

It's true, that leading walks this year has been an immensely fun experience for me. Taking the helm on nearly every outing, I've made a concerted effort to hone my navigational prowess and leadership skills whilst trying to provide entertainment(With sweets and jokes). The recent Grade 3 training session served as a valuable refresher, enabling me to not only sharpen my own abilities but also impart my knowledge to our eager leaders of tomorrow. I’ve made some fond memories with some fluffy cows, and my fellow leaders, contemplating how farmers loose sheep and why is this hill so steep. This year we have been able to train 30 leaders, 12 first aiders, and 5 mini bus drivers. Thanks to all those who helped orchestrate these achievements and to anyone who put themselves forward!

The Joys

Let's cut to the chase: being el presidente is far from a walk in the park. Yet, amidst the challenges, I've been blessed with a trove of cherished memories filled with laughter and pure joy. Whether it's been during our invigorating walks or lively social gatherings, there's been no shortage of Ramsoc inside jokes and intriguing mysteries to unravel. From pondering the identity of the elusive "dino person" to the curious case of my backache post-soft play, and the unexpected appearance of our very own Walter White in Ennerdale, each moment has been a testament to the vibrant spirit of our club. My heartfelt thanks go out to everyone who has joined us on our adventures, embraced the camaraderie, and made every experience truly unforgettable!
